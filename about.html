About

Illustrator &
Motion Designer

My goal is simple: make things more "delicious" and make people happier. I believe good design and good food share the same language. Both can connect people. Whether across the table or across borders, sharing a good meal or a good design, needs no words.

Great recipes bring out the best in its ingredients to make the meal taste better. To do that, I find the right combinations, understand what people enjoy, and choose tools that fit. I approach design the same way. My purpose is clear: to create design that delights, connects and leaves people feeling happy. That is the best kind of flavor.
